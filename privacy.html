Privacy Policy - Canada Residency Tracker

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the app Canada Residency Tracker ("the App") published by Red Panda Studio ("we", "us").

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our own servers. The App does not require an account, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics to track you.

Data Stored on Your Device and in iCloud

Information you enter in the App, such as stay dates, PR card expiry details, and preferences, is stored on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Border Wait Times

When you view border wait times, the App may request publicly available data from third-party or government sources. These requests do not include personally identifiable information.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple handles billing and transaction data under its own privacy policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the App, contact Red Panda Studio at redpandacorp@yahoo.com.
