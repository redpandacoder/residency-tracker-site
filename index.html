Residency Tracker

Track your days in Canada, monitor PR obligations, and check border wait times in one app.

Built for permanent residents, non-residents, and anyone who needs a clear record of physical presence in Canada.

Support Privacy Policy

What the app helps you do

  • Track PR residency obligations over the last 5 years.
  • Monitor the non-resident 183-day rule within the calendar year.
  • Log departures and returns with a simple stay history.
  • Check live Canada-US land border wait times.
  • Back up and sync your data with iCloud on Premium.

Availability

Residency Tracker is available for iPhone and supports English and Simplified Chinese.
